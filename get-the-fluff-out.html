Get the Fluff Out

A management-based game designed to raise awareness about animal shelters and promote pet adoption through interactive gameplay.

Overview

This project focuses on educating players about the challenges faced by animal shelters. Through gameplay, players manage incoming animals, resources, and adoption processes, encouraging empathy and awareness around pet adoption.

My Role

As lead designer, I focused on communication, documentation, and system design. I created the Game Design Document, defined gameplay mechanics, and ensured alignment within the team.

Design Process

The project combined research, system design, and user experience thinking. The gameplay loop was structured around decision-making, resource management, and player feedback systems.

Design Document

Gameplay System

Players manage incoming animals, deciding how to treat and allocate resources. NPCs arrive to adopt pets, requiring players to match preferences efficiently. Reputation is influenced by player decisions, creating a feedback-driven system.
