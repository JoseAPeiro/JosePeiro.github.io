Plush Down

Multiplayer party shooter focused on fast-paced arena gameplay and playful competitive interactions.

Overview

Plush Down is a multiplayer arena shooter designed for up to four players. The game combines simple controls with chaotic mechanics such as bouncing bullets and varied game modes like King of the Hill and trivia challenges.

My Role

I worked as a Game Designer, focusing on research, gameplay systems, and documentation. I developed the Game Design Document, defined mechanics, and created frameworks such as SWOT and Lean Canvas.

Design Process

The project started with research and planning, followed by system design and documentation. Structured frameworks helped guide feature prioritisation and team alignment.
