The Big Dive

Underwater adventure game where players control a submarine using mobile motion controls, combining exploration and combat mechanics.

Overview

The Big Dive is an underwater experience where players navigate a submarine using their phone as a controller. Movement is controlled through the gyroscope, while additional mechanics include shooting enemies and escaping dynamic events such as an attacking octopus.

My Role

I worked as the principal game designer, focusing on environment and level design. My responsibilities included creating documentation (GDD, SWOT, reports), implementing assets in Unity, and designing gameplay systems such as enemy behaviour and flowcharts.

Design Process

After completing the ideation and documentation phase, I focused on structuring gameplay systems. I created flowcharts to visualise interactions, player behaviour, and AI systems, ensuring clarity for both development and design decisions.
