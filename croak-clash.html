Croak Clash

Multiplayer party game focused on competitive minigames, designed to create engaging and fast-paced player interactions.

Overview

Croak Clash is a swamp-themed multiplayer game where players compete in various minigames such as King of the Hill, Capture the Flag, and Tag. Each match consists of multiple rounds where players aim to accumulate the highest score and win the overall session.

My Role

I worked as the lead designer, focusing on communication, documentation, and gameplay systems. I was responsible for maintaining the Game Design Document (GDD), defining mechanics, and coordinating with both the team and external stakeholders.

Design Process

The project began with ideation and documentation, where I structured the gameplay systems and defined the core loop. I created flowcharts and design documentation to ensure alignment within the team and clarity in development.
